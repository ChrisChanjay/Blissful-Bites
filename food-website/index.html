Our Pastry Shop's Journey

It started with a group of students who likes to create their favorite pastries in their spare time. Experimenting new recipes and techniques to improve. As word goes by about the students scrumptious delicacies. They decided to created a bakery shop named it "Blissful Bites". Hospitality is at the heart of the Blissful Bites experience.
We believe a respectful, empathetic working environment is crucial to creating a positive team experience and keeping our guest happy.

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our login hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 Am - 10:00 PM Eastern Time. Outside of these hours, you can still browse our resources and information, but active account management and support are available during our specified hours. Thank you for your understanding!

how do i get my refund?

To initiate your refund, please follow these steps: 1. Log in to your account on our website/app. 2. Navigate to the 'My Orders' section. 3. Click on 'Request Refund' and follow the prompts. Our team will review your request promptly. Thank you!

How long it will take food to arrive?

Food delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes, depending on current demand and your location. We strive to get your meal to you as quickly as possible!

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, our restaurant offers a variety of options including both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes to cater to different preferences.

What is cost of food delivery?

Food delivery costs vary based on distance and order size. Our fees start at ₱38.00 and increase with distance beyond 5 miles. Please check our app or website for specific rates. Thank you!

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Pro membership is available to frequent users who subscribe to our premium services. Enjoy exclusive benefits like advanced features and priority support.
